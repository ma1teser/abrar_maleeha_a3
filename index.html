About

From a young age, pencils, crayons and colourful paper riddled my workspace, yearning for my small hands to experiment and let my imagination shine on the pieces of paper. I found great joy in expressing my creativity and creating unique works of art which influenced me to hold visual art as a hobby through my childhood, teenage years, and now into adulthood. Visual art has always been a major influence in my life as I grew up. It helped me express my creativity, while also giving me a sense of accomplishment when I was able to complete a work of art. It also allowed me to express myself, as I was able to create pieces that reflected my own unique style and vision.
